Academic Departments

The faculty and staff of the College of Sciences.


The College of Sciences offers students a scientific education to meet the needs of the 21st century, allowing them to compete for a variety of careers in an increasingly complex and evolving world.

Biology and Environmental Science

Through teaching, research and service, the Department of Biology and Environmental Science seeks to advance understanding of the sciences at all levels, from molecules to ecosystems. Biology education at AUM strives to strengthen core disciplinary knowledge and to foster synergies that arise from the relationships students build with our creative and collaborative faculty.

Chemistry

The Department of Chemistry offers Bachelor’s degrees in Chemistry (General) and a Professional Chemistry option.  These programs provide excellent preparation for jobs in chemical industry, application to medical, pharmacy, dental and optometry professional schools as well as graduate school in Chemistry.  You can earn the qualifications to become a Forensic Scientist.  Students also have the opportunity to participate in exciting chemical research with our faculty members.  

Mathematics and Computer Science

The Mathematics and Computer Science Department offers exciting and high quality bachelor (in both Mathematics and Computer Science) and master (in Computer Science and Computer Information Systems & Cyber Security) degree programs. We also offer an undergraduate degree in secondary education with an emphasis in Mathematics through cooperation with the College of Education. In addition, the department also offers two minors one in Mathematics and one in Computer Science, and a pre-engineering program.


Psychology

The psychology major offers students a robust education in scientific psychology. Some students will use the major as a stepping-stone to graduate school in psychology.  Others will use it as preparation for law school or a future career in education, counseling or business.  In addition, our research labs can help you develop essential critical thinking and data analysis skills.

Giving to the College of Sciences

The College of Sciences (COS) at AUM has a mission to produce an elite alumni body of talented scientists. Many students possess the talent and drive to join that alumni body but lack the means to realize the full potential of their talent. Gifts from the Friends of the College of Sciences can help students realize their full potential. If you would like to support AUM’s COS mission, please consider donating into a scholarship fund. If you are unable to support a scholarship fund at this time, consider a smaller donation into the COS discretionary fund. Small donations add up and often make the difference for a student in need.
