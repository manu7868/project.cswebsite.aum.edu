A Message from the Dean

The COVID-19 pandemic is unprecedented. The College of Sciences and the AUM community is prepared to assist students in achieving their academic goals. For questions regarding a course, please contact your professors.  For assistance with advising and registration, contact central advising and/or your department chair.  Email addresses and office phones are here http://www.aum.edu/people-finder.  Read the full Message from the Dean!


The College of Sciences offers students a scientific education to meet the needs of the 21st century, allowing them to compete for a variety of careers in an increasingly complex and evolving world.

Academic Programs

The College of Sciences is one of the most comprehensive and diverse academic units in terms of its inventory of degree offerings at AUM. Students can graduate with a degree in Psychology, Mathematics, Computer Science, Chemistry, Biology, Environmental Science, and Geographical Information Systems.

Giving to the College of Sciences

The College of Sciences (COS) at AUM has a mission to produce an elite alumni body of talented scientists. Many students possess the talent and drive to join that alumni body but lack the means to realize the full potential of their talent. Gifts from the Friends of the College of Sciences can help students realize their full potential. If you would like to support AUM’s COS mission, please consider donating into a scholarship fund.
