Academic Programs

The College of Sciences is dedicated to students and supports their pursuit of excellence by providing programs in Psychology, Mathematics, Computer Science, Chemistry, Biology, Environmental Science, and GIS.

The College of Sciences offers students a scientific education to meet the needs of the 21st century, allowing them to compete for a variety of careers in an increasingly complex and evolving world.
